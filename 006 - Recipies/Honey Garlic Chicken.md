# Ingredients
## Chicken
1 lb Chicken Thighs (Cubed)
2 tsp Canola Oil
2 tbsp Cornstarch
Salt + Pepper

## Sauce
1.5 tbsp Low Sodium Soy Sauce
3 tbsp Honey
2 tbsp Water
1/2 tbsp Sugar
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp paprika
3 garlic cloves (minced)

2 tbsp green onion whites
1 Cube of Butter

# Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients for the chicken and mix until coated
2. In a separate bowl, mix all sauce ingredients and whisk
3. Cook chicken until golden brown, then remove from pan
4. Add green onion whites and melt butter in pan
5. Add sauce and let reduce
6. Add chicken and cook on low
