# Ingredients
## Beef
1LB Ground Beef
Salt + Pepper
## Rice
1 Cup Jasmine Rice
1 Cup Water
## Sauce:
1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
3 Garlic Cloves
1 tsp Sesame Oil
1/4 Cup Brown Sugar

# Instructions:
1. Cook ground beef on medium to high heat
2. Mix sauce ingredients in a bowl and whisk
3. Once beef is almost fully cooked, add sauce to pan
4. Add salt and pepper
5. Add rice, green onion and an egg
