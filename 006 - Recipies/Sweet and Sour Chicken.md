# Ingredients
## Chicken
1.5 lb Chicken Breast
2 Eggs
1/2 Cup Cornstarch
Pinch of Salt

## Sauce
1/2 Cup Ketchup
2 tsp Rice Vinegar
2 tbsp Sugar
1 tbsp Water
1 tsp Sesame Oil

# Instructions
1. Add all ingredients for the chicken together and mix
2. In a separate bowl, add all sauce ingredients and mix
3. Deep fry chicken until golden brown, remove and fry again
4. In a pan, pour sauce in and cook the chicken on low heat
